# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
A mix of both commercial agri-buyers (farms, FPOs, nurseries) and premium home gardeners seeking high-grade, region-aware organic inputs.

## Product Purpose
Evoking a sense of trust, scientific authority, and premium quality to drive bulk supply inquiries and retail orders for CROPUS Vermicompost INDIA LLP.

## Brand Personality
Traditional, rustic, organic, and humble.

## Anti-references
- A generic, crowded e-commerce site.
- A cold corporate SaaS landing page.
- Overdesigned, animation-heavy marketing sites.

## Design Principles
- **Region-Aware Specificity**: Highlight why local soil needs custom, crop-specific formulations rather than a one-size-fits-all solution.
- **Editorial Restraint**: Focus on premium typography (serif headlines, monospace labels), generous whitespace, and visual balance.
- **Transparent Trust**: Present verified certifications, real ground-level agricultural work, and clear B2B supply specifications.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance with high-contrast text ratios.
- Respect for user motion preferences (no heavy animations, crossfades/instant transitions for reduced motion).
